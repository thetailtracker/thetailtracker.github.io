About Tail Tracker

Tail Tracker is an innovative pet monitoring system that goes beyond basic location tracking. Our advanced technology provides real-time insights into your pet's activities, ensures they stay within designated areas, and offers interactive features for a comprehensive pet care experience.

Features

Activity Tracking

Monitor your pet's daily activities, including exercise levels, rest periods, and play times. Gain insights into their behavior patterns and overall well-being.

Geo-fencing

Set up virtual boundaries for your pet. Receive instant notifications if they venture into restricted areas, ensuring their safety and your peace of mind.

Real-time Feedback

Get immediate updates on your pet's behavior, location, and activity levels. Stay connected with your furry friend, even when you're away.

Interactive Maps

View detailed activity maps showing your pet's movements throughout the day. Identify favorite spots and track their exploration patterns.

Behavior Analysis

Our system learns your pet's normal behavior patterns and alerts you to any significant changes that might indicate health issues or distress.

Customizable Alerts

Set up personalized notifications for various activities or behaviors. Stay informed about important events in your pet's day.
